Sliding Tile Puzzle

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Sliding Tile Puzzle is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the extension and how that information is handled.

Information Collection

Sliding Tile Puzzle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The extension does not collect:

  • Name or contact information
  • Email address
  • Passwords or authentication information
  • Browsing history
  • Website content
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Health information
  • Analytics or usage tracking

Local Storage

To improve the user experience, the extension uses Chrome's local storage to save game-related information, including:

  • Current puzzle layout
  • Selected difficulty
  • Move count
  • Elapsed timer
  • Best scores and statistics

This information is stored exclusively on the user's device. It is never transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Sliding Tile Puzzle does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, remote code, or tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

No user information is sold, rented, transferred, or disclosed to any third party.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes to the extension or applicable requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
