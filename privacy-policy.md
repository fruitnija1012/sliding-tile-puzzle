# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Sliding Tile Puzzle respects your privacy.

## Information We Collect

Sliding Tile Puzzle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The extension does not collect:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Passwords
- Browsing history
- Website content
- Location information
- Analytics data

## Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's local storage only to save:

- Current puzzle
- Selected difficulty
- Move count
- Timer
- Best scores

This information is stored only on your own device and is never transmitted to the developer or any third party.

## Third Parties

Sliding Tile Puzzle does not sell, share, or transfer user data.
